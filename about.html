[ artist statement ]

I was born in Utrecht, the Netherlands. My youth was spent living in Bolivia, Italy, the United States and Peru. At the start of my teenage years my family and I moved back to the Netherlands, where I have lived ever since.

My work is a visualization of my reflection on the human condition, as personally experienced. I focus on the human figure, its shape, positions and facial expressions in order to contemplate this.
I use the human figure as a means to this end, as something to hold onto whilst conveying the deepest emotions I experience.
